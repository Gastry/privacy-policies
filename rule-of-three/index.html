Rule of Three Calculator lets you perform direct proportion calculations and keep a local history on the device.

This version uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google AdMob may collect and share data such as IP address, app and ad interactions, diagnostic information, and device or account identifiers, including the advertising ID when available. The IP address may be used to estimate the general location of the device. This data may be used to show and measure ads, prevent fraud, and manage consent and privacy choices. When required by applicable rules, the app will show a consent form and a way to review your privacy choices.
